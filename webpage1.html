Pizza is a popular dish that originated in Italy. It typically consists of a dough base, tomato sauce, and various toppings such as cheese, meats, and vegetables. Pizza is a versatile dish that can be made in many different ways, from traditional Margherita to more unconventional options like BBQ chicken or Hawaiian.

Burgers are a classic fast food dish that originated in the United States. A burger typically consists of a patty of ground beef, grilled or fried, and served on a bun with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, and sauce. Burgers can also be made with different types of meat such as chicken, turkey, or even plant-based options.
Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish that typically consists of meat that is cooked on a spit and shaved off as it rotates. The meat, usually chicken or lamb, is then placed in a pita bread with various toppings such as tomatoes, onions, and tahini sauce. Shawarma is a popular street food and is often served as a wrap or sandwich.
